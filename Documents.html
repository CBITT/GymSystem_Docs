Gym System

Created by Csaba Bango, Neil Sankar, Peter Mikulesko

Vision Statement

It is a system built for both members and gym administrators. It helps the administrator to keep track of their members, employees and basic operations within the facility. The member can manage his/her membership status and get an overview of the available services that the gym offers.

Use Cases

  • Performed by member
    • Book instructor
    • Group books room
    • Book equipment
    • Check remaining sessions
    • Buy membership
    • Start workout
    • Create account
  • Performed by administrator
    • Add special offers
    • Order equipment
    • Register member
    • Ban Member
    • Unban member
    • Maintain equipment
    • Print basic statistics
    • Calculate salary of employee
    • Order food supplements
  • Performed by both member and administrator
    • Log in
    • Log out
